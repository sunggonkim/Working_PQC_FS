\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

AEGIS-Q is an evidence-first secure edge-storage prototype: secure-storage pressure raises foreground SQLite p99 from 6.4~ms to 13.8~ms with one deadline miss; AEGIS-Q recovers 8.8~ms, zero misses, and 2.7~MB/s background progress.  It does not claim direct NVMe-to-UVM DMA, eBPF/io\_uring completion bypass, GPU constant-time behavior, persistent PCR-bound freshness, power-loss certification, foreground AI p99 recovery, or portability.
